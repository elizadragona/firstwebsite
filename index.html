Online Safety 💻

In our days, the Internet and Social Networks have become a part of our daily lives. We use both to keep our knowledge up to date, inform ourselves about the latest news, communicate with others and much more. The internet offers us unlimited opportunities and it is free. The problem is that many dangers are hidden behind the advantages of the Internet that most people ignore or are not aware of them.

Dangers of the internet:

  • Cyberbullying
  • Viruses
  • Invasion of privacy
  • Identity theft
  • Software Piracy
  • Malware
  • Copyright
